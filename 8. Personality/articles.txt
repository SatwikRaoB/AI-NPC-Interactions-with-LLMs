ARTICLE
Title: The Fine Arc Program Relocates to Utica’s Art District
The Fine Arc Program has officially relocated to Utica’s vibrant art district, establishing a creative hub for disabled artists to showcase and sell their work. The move was celebrated with a ribbon-cutting ceremony at the M building, marking a new chapter for the program’s mission to empower artists with disabilities through community engagement and artistic expression.

ARTICLE
Title: States Resist Trump’s DEI Funding Cuts
Democratic-led states are actively resisting the Trump administration’s threats to cut federal school funding over Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) programs. Some state leaders have vowed to refuse compliance with the proposed cuts, while others are preparing lawsuits to challenge the administration’s authority, highlighting a significant clash over educational policy and inclusivity.
